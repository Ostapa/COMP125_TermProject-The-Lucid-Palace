 Story Of The Palace 

Lucid Palace is growing to become one of the most luxurios hotels in the industry. Because of our innovative approach to products, amenities and service, Lucid Palace continues to be synonymous with hotels across the globe.

The Lucid Palace remains the stylish, forward thinking giant of hospitality – and we help make traveling easier with our smart design, innovative restaurant concepts, authentic hospitality and commitment to the global community.

 A Room For You 

With 3 locations, Lucid Palace provides an authentic and contemporary experience for our guests. Our guest rooms feature a smart, functional design plus thoughtful amenities.

And our Request Upon Arrival service ensures that your room will be unique to your specifications from the moment you check in.

 Food & Drinks 

We know that food and drink are an important part of any stay. Whether dining in one of our innovative on-site restaurants or having a healthy Palace Breakfast in-room, you’ll find delicious and accessible options to satisfy any craving.

In the Lucid Palace you'll find the finest wine list with French, California, and Chilean varietals well represented. The service is top notch: extremely attentive and delicately courteous.

 What Can We Do For You 

Our Team Members are always ready to provide friendly, individualized service to our guests.

We’re also happy to offer a range of services to meet your needs. From booking to checkout, we’re here to make your stay as enjoyable as possible.
